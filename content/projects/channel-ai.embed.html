From raw dumps to a queryable layer

Every source, whatever its origin, lands in Apache Iceberg through Spark ingestion jobs, then gets reshaped into a per-client star schema. Federation falls out for free: one question can join the ERP against the finance spreadsheet against BigQuery.

Dimensional modeling turned out to be model context: a declared grain and a real date dimension improved SQL generation accuracy more than any prompting change. Kimball was writing context engineering thirty years early.

How answers become charts

Chart generation is spec-driven, not free-form plotting code. The analysis agent picks a spec from the result frame's shape; a renderer does the rest. This killed an entire genre of hallucinated-plotting bugs and made every chart reproducible from its spec.
